# AI Infrastructure Research Platform

> Transform fragmented information into structured knowledge and continuously evolving investment hypotheses.

---

# Vision

The goal of this project is to continuously refine the understanding of the AI infrastructure ecosystem and support better long-term investment decisions through structured research.

Rather than predicting the market, the platform focuses on building, validating, and updating investment hypotheses as new evidence emerges.

This platform is designed primarily as a personal research workspace. Every feature should help answer one question:

> **Has anything happened that should change my understanding of the AI infrastructure industry?**

---

# The Problem

AI infrastructure research requires information from many different sources.

Examples include:

- Earnings calls
- Conference presentations
- Research papers
- Financial statements
- Industry reports
- Daily news
- Macroeconomic data

Although these sources contain valuable information, they are fragmented.

The difficult part is not obtaining information—it is connecting information into a coherent research process.

Most existing platforms organize information.

Few platforms organize research.

---

# Product Philosophy

## Facts over Opinions

Facts are immutable.

Reasoning should always be based on verifiable evidence.

The platform should clearly separate:

- Facts
- Evidence
- Reasoning
- Investment Hypotheses

These concepts should never be mixed together.

---

## Explainable Reasoning

Every conclusion should be traceable.

The platform should never simply state that a company or industry benefits.

Instead, every conclusion should include the reasoning process that leads to it.

Research should always remain explainable.

---

## Investment Hypotheses are Living Documents

Investment hypotheses should continuously evolve.

New evidence may:

- strengthen a hypothesis
- weaken a hypothesis
- challenge a hypothesis
- require further investigation

The platform helps organize this process.

The final judgement always belongs to the user.

---

## Research Before Investment

The platform does not attempt to generate buy or sell recommendations.

Instead, it helps identify:

- what deserves further research
- which assumptions should be reviewed
- which industries are changing
- which companies require additional attention

The goal is to improve research quality rather than automate investment decisions.

---

# Research Workflow

Every piece of information should follow the same workflow.

```
News
Conference
Research Paper
Financial Report
Macro Data

        ↓

Summary

        ↓

Fact Extraction

        ↓

Evidence

        ↓

Reasoning

        ↓

Industry Thesis

        ↓

Company Thesis

        ↓

Research Actions

        ↓

Watchlist

        ↓

Portfolio Decisions
```

Only facts should enter the knowledge base.

Reasoning should always remain transparent and explainable.

---

# Long-term Direction

The platform is expected to evolve gradually.

```
Dashboard

↓

Knowledge Base

↓

Research Platform

↓

Research Workspace
```

The objective is not to build another dashboard.

The objective is to build a workspace where investment knowledge accumulates over time.

As knowledge grows, research should become faster, more structured, and more consistent.

---

# Design Principles

When designing new features, always ask:

- Does this improve research quality?
- Does this help connect information?
- Does this make reasoning more transparent?
- Would I actually use this during my own research?

If the answer is no, the feature should be reconsidered.

The platform should assist thinking.

It should never replace thinking.
